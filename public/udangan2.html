Our Story

We met on a rainy afternoon in a crowded coffee shop. Daniel was struggling with his umbrella while Sarah watched with amusement from her corner table. When their eyes met, it felt like time stood still.

Three years later, on a beautiful spring morning during a picnic in the park, Daniel got down on one knee surrounded by blooming flowers. Sarah said "Yes" before he could even finish his sentence.

Now we're excited to begin our forever together, and we'd be honored to have you celebrate this special day with us.
